August 20-21, 2011

Greeley, Colorado

*** Registration has ended ***


The Pilgrimage Experience of the World Youth Day in Madrid, Spain will be connected to the young people of Colorado.


Featuring:

  • Catechese-Lessons in the Pilgrimage Experience offered in local parishes

  • Pilgrimage in unity with the Faith Community as many parishes join together in the journey

  • Musical camaraderie as you, choirs and musicians display individual talents and then join together as one voice in celebration

  • Inspiration by well known Christian Rock Bands

  • Participation in the simultaneous celebration in Madrid, Spain

  • Praying together in awesome adoration of Jesus in the Eucharist

  • Sharing our Faith with fellow pilgrims in a sunrise celebration of the Liturgy of the Mass

  • Going forth as Disciples returning form a pilgrimage

Pilgrims will:

  • Prepare for the pilgrimage with lessons and activities

  • Gather to walk in pilgrimage to the District 6 Stadium

  • Enjoy the music of two nationally known Christian Rock Bands 7Eventh Time Downand Building 429

  • Participate in Activities and Meet other young people

  • Meet Jesus in a large outdoor group adoration

  • Enjoy a satellite feed of the Mass with the Holy Father in Madrid, Spain

  • Attend a Sunrise vigil service and concelebrated Mass

  • Enjoy a great pancake breakfast at dawn

  • Always remember the power of Young Catholics joined in prayer

Planted and built up in Jesus Christ, Firm in Faith
