Ticket Info

Regstration is due by August 1, 2011

Goal

To offer an affordable alternative for parishes to send their young people to Greeley, Colorado and experience a taste of World Youth Day. Ages 13 to 35 years old. Attendees under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult.
Each participating parish may send as many pilgrims as they would like for a flat fee.The amount of the fee is based upon the size of the parish.

  • Less than 200 families    $500
  • 200-500 families               $750
  • 501-1000 families         $1,000
  • 1001 and more              $2,000

To attend without going through a parish or youth ministry, please fill out an individual or family registration form

  • Individual and College Student    $40 (For Concert ONLY)
  • Family      $100 (For Concert ONLY)

Checks can be sent to:
