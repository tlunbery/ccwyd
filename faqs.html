Frequently Asked Questions


What is The Colorado Connection to World Youth Day?

Celebrate the World Youth Day with the Catholic Youth pilgrims from around the world as they gather in Madrid, Spain

Where is it located?

Greeley, Colorado at the Weld County District 6 Stadium

Why should I come?

It is an affordable alternate way of participating in one of the great events of our Faith tradition


Who is eligible to attend?

Young people who are entering grade 7 or above (attendees under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult), Youth Ministers, Pastors, etc.

When is it held?

August 20-21, 2011 begins on Saturday at 3pm ends Sunday morning

How much does it cost cost?

Sliding scale for Parishes, $40 for an Individual or College Student (For Concert ONLY), $100 for a family (For Concert ONLY)
