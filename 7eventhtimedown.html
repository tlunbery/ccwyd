7Eventh Time Down

Bio

7eventh Time Down is a group of life long friends from southern Kentucky, whose mission is to quite literally change the world. Their fresh, mainstream, pop/rock sound comes from an incredible blend of heavy guitars, and soaring melodies from a larger than life lead vocal. They are a group of �no bones about it� believers and followers of the teachings of Jesus Christ, and their lyrics reflect this. Their goal is to set the world on fire with the idea of �living for something bigger than yourself�. They have been touring together for 7 years, cultivating and developing their sound, while sharing the stage with such names as Skillet, Kutless, Decemberadio, Pillar, Newsong, Disciple, Fireflight, Stellar Kart, Family Force 5, Decyfer Down, Jars of Clay, KJ-52, and many more.

Members

Mikey Howard (Lead Vocals / Rthm Guitar), Eric VanZant (Lead Guitar / Bkg Vocals), Austin Miller (Drums), Cliff Williams (Bass / Bkg Vocals)
